II

The Cyber Magus

Technology is woven into everything I do. I’m constantly surrounded by it, whether through coding, digital tools, or just my general tech-savvy lifestyle. The Cyber Magus represents how I harness technology to shape my identity and create my world. It’s about mastering digital spaces and making them work for me, just as I navigate and control the data that comes from my online presence.

Upright Meaning

Mastery: You are in control of your digital and technological tools, using them to empower yourself and those around you. This card represents innovation and forward-thinking.

Connection: Your networks are strong, and you are effectively harnessing technology to create or expand your influence.

Empowerment: You are resourceful and adaptive, mastering both the virtual and real worlds in a way that benefits your goals.

Reversed Meaning

Overdependence: You may be relying too much on technology, leading to disconnection from the physical world or real relationships.

Isolation: Social media or digital tools are causing isolation instead of connection. It’s time to reconnect on a more personal level.

Misuse: There is a danger of using technology for manipulation or control. Be mindful of how you wield power over others or allow technology to control you.
