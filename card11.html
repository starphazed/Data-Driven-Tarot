Upright Meaning

Perspective: You are seeing the world through many lenses, and this ability to shift perspectives is giving you a broader, more colorful understanding of life.

Creativity: Your creativity is flourishing, and you are able to see connections and patterns in new ways. This is a time of artistic or innovative growth.

Transformation: Life is constantly shifting, and you are embracing the beauty in change. You are adaptable and open to new experiences.

Reversed Meaning

Confusion: You are overwhelmed by too many perspectives, making it difficult to find clarity. You may be feeling lost in a sea of shifting ideas.

Fragmentation: Your focus is scattered, and you are struggling to piece together a cohesive vision or plan.

Stagnation: Despite the changing patterns around you, you feel stuck. You may be resisting the flow of transformation.
