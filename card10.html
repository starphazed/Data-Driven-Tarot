The Resonance

Everything I do has an impact, even if it’s not always obvious. The Resonance symbolizes the vibrations and energy I put out into the world, both in real life and through my digital actions. My data is a record of these echoes, showing how what I do ripples outward, touching others in ways I may not immediately see.

Upright Meaning

Vibration: Everything you do sends out ripples. You are in a state of alignment, and your energy is resonating deeply with those around you.

Connection: There is a deep emotional or spiritual connection at play. You are in tune with the world around you, and the vibrations you send out are positive and uplifting.

Legacy: Your actions now will have a lasting impact. What you put out into the world will continue to resonate long after you’re gone.

Reversed Meaning

Disharmony: There is a disconnect between your intentions and the results. You may be out of sync with those around you or with your environment.

Distortion: Your message or actions are being misinterpreted, causing confusion or misunderstandings.

Invisibility: You may feel like your voice or efforts are going unnoticed. Despite your efforts, you’re not seeing the resonance you hoped for.
