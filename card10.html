The Resonance

Everything I do has an impact, even if it’s not always obvious. The Resonance symbolizes the vibrations and energy I put out into the world, both in real life and through my digital actions. My data is a record of these echoes, showing how what I do ripples outward, touching others in ways I may not immediately see.

Connection: There is a deep emotional or spiritual connection at play. You are in tune with the world around you, and the vibrations you send out are positive and uplifting. Legacy: Your actions now will have a lasting impact. What you put out into the world will continue to resonate long after you’re gone.
